TCE

Thiagarajar College of Engineering (TCE), Madurai, founded by Late. Shri. Karumuttu Thiagarajan Chettiar was established in 1957. The college is affiliated to Anna University. It has been approved by AICTE and accredited by NAAC. The college aims to impart world class technical education with string technical values. Along with the objective of imparting knowledge, the college has the vision of promoting research, fostering cooperation and exchange of ideas between industrial organizations and the academic community and the development of entrepreneurship skills among the students.
